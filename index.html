Who Am I

about me

Hi, I'm Charmie. I run this site with a group of independent musicians and writers.

We're not owned or affiliated with any mainstream media organization or record label. Everything is written by us from a place of love for the music, and I hope you can read that in what we write.

Our mission is to share great music for the benefit of all. In a world that's becoming ever more polarized, we believe music can be a force for good. Thanks for stopping by.
